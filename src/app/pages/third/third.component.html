The analysis focuses on predicting the likelihood of product reorders based on user purchase behavior. By examining past order data, we can estimate the probability of a user repurchasing specific products, enabling more precise stock management. This predictive model uses historical purchase data to anticipate future demand, allowing businesses to tailor their inventory and marketing strategies accordingly.

Raising Awareness

The analysis highlights distinct behaviors that can raise awareness about sustainable shopping. Businesses can target Cluster 1, which prefers packaged products, with educational campaigns on the environmental impact of packaging and waste reduction. Sharing relevant information can encourage this group to adopt more responsible habits.

Encouraging Eco-Friendly Purchasing

To encourage eco-friendly behaviors, businesses can target Cluster 1 with incentives like discounts for choosing fresh, packaging-free products. Improving the visibility and accessibility of eco-friendly options through store layouts and promotions can also help shift purchasing habits toward more sustainable choices
